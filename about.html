About Us

Interact stands for INTERtional ACTion. Interact is a youth service organization that offers volunteering opportunities and brings together high school students ages 12-18. Interact spans the world with more than 14,000 clubs worldwide in 145 countries and is affiliated with Rotary International.

In Interact, we can make more than a difference. Beyond accumulating service hours, we are here to make a difference in the community and help each individual to develop leadership and personal integrity. At Dunlap Interact Club, we are here to develop leadership and personal integrity, take action in the school and community, network and build connections in community, and advance community understanding and goodwill.

Join us at our monthly club meetings on Thursdays. Access the Remind at the link above for updates and announcements. In the Google Drive, find the event calendar and monthly overviews if you missed a club meeting.

Our club sponsor is Mr. Olson, whose classroom is located in N14.

What We Do

Each year, we have two service projects: one to help serve the community and another to promote international understanding. If you have any suggestions for future project ideas, feel free to leave a message on the Contact Page. We'll get back to you as soon as possible!
